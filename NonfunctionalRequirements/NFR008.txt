REQ-ID: NFR008
Description: A receptionist shall be able to enter a time period reasonably quickly
Type: Usability
Rationale: The receptionist will need to find an appropriate date for the patient’s next appointment without delay
Fit Criterion: Eighty percent of a panel that is representative of the receptionists will be able to enter a specified number and select a specified time period within 15 seconds.
